How It Works

From file to insight in three steps.

No configuration, no SQL, no dashboarding tool. Just upload, ask, and analyze.

01
Upload your dataset
Drop a CSV, XLSX, Parquet, or JSON file. The quality pipeline runs instantly — schema detection, missing-value analysis, and a quality report, all automatic.
02
Ask a question
Type your question in plain language — "Show revenue trends for Q3" or "Which category has the most missing values?" The LangGraph agent interprets intent and builds a validated plan.
03
Get your analysis
Receive text answers, bar charts, line graphs, histograms, scatter plots, or full multi-panel dashboards — all rendered deterministically and persisted in the chat history.
